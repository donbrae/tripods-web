Item, for the eschewyn of maneswering of false inquestis and assisis in gret hurtyn of oure soverane lordis leigis, and specialy be the inquestis in thar heretage, it is statut and ordanit that in tym cummyne quhar a party findis him grevit be ony assise or inquestis be partial, malice or ignorance of the assise or the inquest, saffand and excepand the assisis of brevis pledabile quhilk this statute sal nocht extend apoune, it salbe leful to the said partii grevit to cum to oure soverane lorde or his consal and tak a summondis of the said inquest to compeir befor thaim a certane day and place perempturly, and thar produce his avidentis of the ignorance or falste of the said inquest.
